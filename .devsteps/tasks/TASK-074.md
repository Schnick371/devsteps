### Design Decisions

**Step Dots Metaphor:**
- Literal "DevSteps" visualization (steps = progress milestones)
- Simplest geometry of all 3 concepts
- Universal progression symbol
- Works at any scale (28px to 128px)

**AI Lens Concept:**
- Circle = AI analytical viewport (magnification/focus)
- Perfect containment (no extending elements)
- Clean rendering at small sizes
- Double meaning: lens AND glasses reference

**Opacity Strategy:**
- Completed: 0.25-0.5 (faded past)
- Current: 0.85-0.95 (highlighted focus)
- Future: 0.2-0.4 (subtle roadmap)
- Mimics real workflow board visual hierarchy

**Path Layout Variations:**
- A: Linear horizontal (direct progression)
- B: Curved organic (natural workflow)
- C: Connection-emphasized (relationships)
- D: 3D depth (journey perspective)

**Technical Specs Met:**
- ✅ 28×28 size with 24×24 viewBox
- ✅ Circle: 2px (A/B/D), 1.8px (C minimal)
- ✅ Dots: 1.2-3px radius (size = emphasis)
- ✅ Connections: 0.8-2px (thicker in C)
- ✅ currentColor throughout (perfect theme compatibility)
- ✅ All elements within circle (contained design)

### Advantages Over Other Concepts

**vs Concept 1 (Kanban + Glasses):**
- Simpler geometry (dots vs lanes+cards)
- Methodology-agnostic (not Scrum-specific)
- Faster recognition at 28px

**vs Concept 2 (Footsteps):**
- Better small-size rendering (solid shapes vs outlines)
- More abstract (broader interpretation)
- Contained (no extending elements)

### Performance

**File Sizes:**
- Variant A: 1.3KB (simplest)
- Variant B: 1.6KB (curved path)
- Variant C: 1.2KB (minimal, smallest)
- Variant D: 1.8KB (most elements)

All optimized for fast rendering at small sizes.

### Recommendations

**Best for Activity Bar (28×28):**
- Variant A or C (clearest at tiny size)

**Best for Marketplace (128×128):**
- Variant B or D (detail emerges at scale)

**Accessibility:**
- Works for color-blind users (opacity only)
- Clear geometric shapes (no ambiguity)
- High contrast between current and other steps
