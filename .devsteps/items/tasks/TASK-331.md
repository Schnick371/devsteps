coord writes a dispatch-manifest.json to .devsteps/cbp/&lt;sprint_id&gt;/dispatch-manifest.json when dispatching Ring 1 agents. Schema: { schema_version, sprint_id, triage_tier, session_start, dispatches: [{ mandate_id, agent, ring, dispatched_at, completed_at, duration_ms, status, confidence, output_tokens_approx }] }. Use expected_agent_names[] not expected_mandate_ids[] (UUIDs unknown at dispatch time). coord patches the manifest on read_mandate_results return. Enables audit trail and timeout detection.
