### Kategorien

1. **Side-Quest Pattern** — Items entdecken ohne Flow-Unterbrechung (flag, split)
2. **Quality Tracking** — Acceptance Criteria, Tech-Debt, Test Evidence
3. **Human-AI Collaboration** — Async Q&A, Session Digest, Human Notes
4. **System Intelligence** — Conflict Detection, Duplicate Detection, Batch Operations
5. **Effort & Progress** — Velocity Tracking, Blocked-Item Surfacing
