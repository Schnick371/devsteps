Implement the Spider Web Visualizer as a D3.js polar ring diagram showing all 6 rings (Ring 0–5) and all 31 registered agents. Active agents are highlighted in real-time based on JSONL trace events from the FSWatcher pipeline. The card mini-preview shows the current/last agent activation as a plain SVG. The detail tab shows the full D3 diagram with hover tooltips and a session sidebar listing the last 10 dispatches. Requires a second esbuild entry point for the D3 browser bundle (~120KB). Depends on S2 (TraceLogger) and the Spike result. Affected: packages/extension/src/webview/, packages/extension/esbuild.js
